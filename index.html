Salesforce Tutorial – A Complete Guide for Beginners

Welcome to the complete Salesforce Tutorial. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with Salesforce, from basic concepts to advanced development.

Salesforce is a cloud-based software company that provides a platform for businesses to manage customer relationships, sales, and operations.

What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based software company that provides its customers with a platform to develop their own applications without following the tough steps that they used to follow in the legacy system.

It is currently the number one Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform in the world, used by companies of all sizes to connect with their customers.

What is a Salesforce Administrator?

A Salesforce Administrator is a business leader who knows how to use the features and functionality of the platform to solve business problems.

They are responsible for managing users, data, security, and building automation within the Salesforce environment.

Salesforce Lightning Components

Salesforce Lightning is a modern user interface framework developed by Salesforce. It is designed to help developers create enterprise applications faster.

It provides out-of-the-box components that make building apps easier than with the classic interface.

Salesforce Marketing Cloud

Marketing Cloud is a digital marketing platform that allows businesses to create and manage marketing relationships and campaigns with customers.

It supports email, social media, and mobile marketing, as well as data analysis and audience management.

Salesforce CRM

Salesforce CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a technology for managing all your company’s relationships and interactions with customers and potential customers.

It helps improve profitability by streamlining processes and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Salesforce Architecture

The architecture of Salesforce is designed to be scalable, secure, and multi-tenant. It consists of different layers:

  • Multi-tenant Architecture: Multiple customers share the same infrastructure.
  • Metadata-driven: The behavior of the application is driven by metadata.
  • API Access: Everything in Salesforce is accessible via APIs.

Creating a Salesforce Developer Account

Creating a free Developer Edition (DE) account is the first step for learning Salesforce.

1. Go to developer.salesforce.com

2. Click Sign Up.

3. Fill in your details and verify your email.

Salesforce Data Types

Salesforce supports various data types for fields:

  • Text: Alphanumeric characters.
  • Number: Numeric values.
  • Date/Time: Calendar dates and times.
  • Picklist: A list of options to choose from.
  • Checkbox: Boolean values (True/False).

Creating a Salesforce Application

An app in Salesforce is a logical container for a set of tabs, objects, and other items that work together to solve a specific business problem.

You can create apps using the App Manager in Setup.

Salesforce AppExchange

AppExchange is Salesforce's marketplace for business applications. It's like an "App Store" for your CRM.

You can find thousands of pre-built apps and components to extend Salesforce functionality.

Salesforce Objects Tutorial

Objects are essentially database tables in Salesforce. They store data.

Standard Objects: Pre-built objects like Account, Contact, Lead.

Custom Objects: Objects you create to store information specific to your organization.

Creating Custom Fields

Custom fields allow you to capture specific data points on objects.

Navigate to: Object Manager > [Select Object] > Fields & Relationships > New.

Reports and Dashboards

Reports: Lists of data based on specific criteria.

Dashboards: Visual representations of report data, showing key metrics at a glance.

Wrapper Class in Salesforce

A wrapper class is a custom class defined by a developer to display different objects on a Visualforce page or Lightning component.

It acts as a container to hold multiple data types in a single unit.

Governor Limits in Salesforce

Because Salesforce is a multi-tenant environment, it enforces limits to ensure no single organization monopolizes shared resources.

Examples include limits on SOQL query rows (50,000), CPU time, and heap size.

Process Builder

Process Builder is a point-and-click tool that lets you easily automate if/then business processes.

It is more powerful than Workflow Rules and allows for multiple actions in a single process.

Workbench in Salesforce

Workbench is a powerful, web-based suite of tools designed for administrators and developers to interact with Salesforce organizations.

It is commonly used to run SOQL queries, REST API calls, and migrate data.

Batch Apex

Batch Apex is used to process large numbers of records that would exceed normal processing limits.

It breaks the processing into manageable chunks to avoid hitting Governor Limits.

Workflow in Salesforce

Workflow rules automate standard internal procedures and processes to save time across your organization.

They consist of: 1) Criteria (If), 2) Actions (Then).

Triggers in Salesforce

Apex triggers enable you to perform custom actions before or after events to records in Salesforce, such as insertions, updates, or deletions.

Syntax: `trigger TriggerName on ObjectName (trigger_events) { ... }`

Salesforce Lightning Tutorial

Lightning Experience is the modern, fast, and beautiful user interface designed for Salesforce.

It offers new features, improved design, and better performance compared to Salesforce Classic.

Salesforce Cheat Sheet

Quick reference guide for common Salesforce tasks:

  • Global Search: Magnifying glass icon.
  • Create New: The "Create" dropdown in the top right.
  • Setup: Gear icon > Setup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Salesforce free?
A: It has a free Developer Edition for learning, but production licenses are paid.

Q: What language does Salesforce use?
A: Apex (similar to Java) for backend, and Lightning Web Components (JavaScript) for frontend.
